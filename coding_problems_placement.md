## 💡 Quick Tips

- **Arrays & Strings** → Always think Two Pointers or Sliding Window first
- **Sets** → Use when you need uniqueness or fast lookup
- **Maps** → Use when you need to count, group, or find pairs
- **Recursion** → Always define the base case before writing recursive call
- **Time limit:** Aim to solve Easy problems in **10–15 min**, Medium in **25–30 min**
